About

Not the usual
designer story.

My background is in Electrical and Electronics — Jain Polytechnic, Belgaum. Not design school. But even back then, I was recording and editing videos on my phone. Just out of curiosity, just because I loved it. That spark is what pulled me toward creativity.

Shifting from technical to creative was entirely my own call. That structural mind is what makes the work different — I think in systems before I think in aesthetics.
